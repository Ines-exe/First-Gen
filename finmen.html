Financial Aid

Fafsa

Fafsa or the Free Application for Federal Student Aid, is a form avaible to students whichs offers them financial aid. There are many forms of financial aid such as federal grants, subsidized loans or unsubsidized federal loans. Which aid a student gets is determined by a students eligibility for need-based and non-need-based aid.
